Eject All App: A statusbar application to eject all external disks on your Mac
November 13, 2016
Menu interface of this simple app.

This is a simple statusbar app to quickly eject all eligible disks in two clicks. I created it as an alternative method when I'm not connected to the USB hub with the Physical Eject All button.

Notification with sound when eject is successful.
