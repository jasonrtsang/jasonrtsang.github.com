Hi I'm Jason, an eager Computer Engineer studying on the mountain of Simon Fraser University, located in Greater Vancouver.

I love clean minimalistic design, but much like housing in Vancouver, I can't afford the costs associated with it (getting less but costing more?!?). Hence, welcome to my internet home assembled on the free hosting of GitHub. Inspired by Squarespace's Native template, this site is written with simple HTML and CSS. Oh and the most minuscule bit of JavaScript for displaying my name banner designs (try refreshing this page if you haven't noticed it). Those fonts are from Fontfabric. Big fan of their work, so I couldn't just settle on one.

As this site is a public repository, feel free to take snippets from its source code. You can check out its project here . Hosting on GitHub has additional benefits too! I don't need to worry about backups and security and its uptime and speeds are impeccable! However, it only supports static assets (HTML, CSS, JS) which are perfectly ample for me.
